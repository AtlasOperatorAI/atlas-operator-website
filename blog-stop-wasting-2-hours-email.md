## Why Email Takes So Long

Three reasons:

**1. You sort manually**
Every email that lands in your inbox requires a decision: Important? Can ignore? Needs response? That's a decision you make hundreds of times per day. Decisions are expensive. They drain your brain.

**2. You read everything**
Not everything in your inbox matters. Most of it is noise. But you read it anyway because you're afraid you'll miss something. So you read 47 emails to find the 3 that matter. That's 94% waste.

**3. You respond to urgency, not importance**
The squeaky wheel gets the grease. So you respond to emails that yell (all caps, multiple exclamation marks) instead of emails that matter. Your best customers don't yell. They're patient. They wait while you respond to the noise.

## The Solution (It's Simpler Than You Think)

You don't need to hire someone. You need to automate the sorting.

Here's what actually happens when you put an AI between you and your inbox:

**Every morning at 7 AM**, your inbox gets sorted automatically into 3 buckets:

- **URGENT** (2-3 items): Client emergencies, time-sensitive requests, things that actually need you today
- **NEEDS YOU** (5-8 items): Decisions, approvals, responses, follow-ups that require your input
- **FYI** (everything else): Newsletters, notifications, confirmations, updates—archived so they don't clutter your view
